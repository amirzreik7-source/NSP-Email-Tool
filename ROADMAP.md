## Phase 7: Strategy Hub + Design Hub + Unified Flow ✅
- **Feature 0: Tracking & Analytics** — Global tracking settings, open pixel, unique link tracking, website visit tracking, per-campaign overrides
- **Feature 1: Strategy Hub** — Conversational AI marketing strategist replaces home screen. One smart recommendation. Long-term objectives with progress tracking. Full business context assembly.
- **Feature 2: Design Hub** — Interactive email design through conversation. 3 email styles (Pure Personal, Soft Branded, Campaign). Live preview with desktop/mobile toggle.
- **Feature 3: Unified Campaign Flow** — 6-step continuous flow: Strategy → Audience → Content → Samples → Design → Confirm. Draft saving. Optimal send time suggestions.
- **List Intelligence Upgrades** — Fuzzy contact matching (name+city, name+address). Data conflict flagging. Confidence scoring per data point.
- **Sidebar Update** — Strategy Hub replaces Today as home. Campaigns removed from sidebar (launched from Strategy Hub).

## Phase 8: Data Foundations + UX Restructure + Smart Campaign Flow ✅
- **Data Foundations:** Campaign delivery log per contact, source tracking, Storm Score on every contact (daily calc), coordinates at import, overlap detection on upload, Mark as Won
- **Sender Profiles:** Full profiles for Amir & Mary with signatures and writing style, injected into AI generation
- **UX Restructure:** New sidebar (Home, Lead Finder, Contacts, Conversations, Reports, Settings). Lead Finder scoped to net-new leads with validation inbox. Contacts gets 4 tabs (All, Lists, Hot Contacts, Nearby).
- **Campaign Flow Rebuild:** 4 audience paths (list, nearby, high-priority, AI suggest). Exclusion filters (by list, city, engagement, specific contact, days since emailed). Overlap detection. Strategy chat inline. Campaign Brief auto-fills from AI JSON. 3-sample preview before full generation.
- **Storm Score Visibility:** Contact profiles, contact table (sortable), list cards, Hot Contacts tab, campaign flow audience filter, AI context
- **Fixes:** Strategy brief carry-over (structured JSON), navigation buttons, Lead Finder rename
